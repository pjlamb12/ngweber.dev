Welcome to ngWeber!

ngWeber is an Angular meetup located in the Weber and Davis county area of Northern Utah. Angular is a great framework with a great community of developers. ngWeber will allow members of this community to gather together to learn new things, make new friends, and support each other. Any and all are welcome. Please connect with us on Twitter, Spectrum, or at our YouTube channel if you have any questions!

Goals

ngWeber has a few specific goals: support Angular developers in the community, learn new topics and practice old topics, and make new friends.

To accomplish these goals, ngWeber will meet together often. Many of those meetings will be held online, primarily using YouTube live. Go check out ngHouston or AngularAir for examples of this. We will schedule meetings and allow as many people to watch live as possible. Each recorded episode will be immediately available on YouTube, and then will be converted to an audio podcast and be released on many podcast platforms. Check out our Anchor page for more information on the podcast.

Thanks, and Welcome!

With your help, we can make ngWeber the best meetup in town, and will be able to benefit many developers in the area. If you'd like to get involved, please reach out via DM on Twitter or Spectrum. You can submit requess for episodes at those locations or volunteer to present. If you're a new presenter just looking to get into public speaking, let us know and you can get your start and some practice here. Thanks again!
